The Digital Dreamgirl Diary is a digital community to speak out, be heard, and feel seen. This online archive shares real stories from women, non-binary and femme-identifying people about their experiences with Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV).The Digital Dreamgirl Diary is a digital community to speak out, be heard, and feel seen.

Technology-Facilitated Gender-Based Violence (TFGBV) is defined as any form of gendered abuse carried out, amplified, or enabled through digital technology. (eSafety Commissioner Australia, 2024)

When published on the ... 2025, 90% of individuals who completed this survey experienced some form of gender-based harassment through digital technology. Research shows TFGBV is a predecessor to physical abuse.

This project aims raises awareness of how digital spaces, algorithmic bias, and the growing influence of the manosphere shapes gendered harm through your diverse experiences over time.

The Digital Dreamgirl Diary is an archival, ongoing project that observes how tech-driven violence has evolved through generations alongside social media and AI. It is open for contributions via the link below, you may submit more than once. All submissions are anonymous and screened for hate speech, discrimination and threats before publication.

Your privacy and protection is highest priority.
